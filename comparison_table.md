## Notes

1. **NM prices** are for 2025-2026 license year. NM legislative session in 2025 passed NR fee increases effective April 2026.
2. **CO 2028 change**: Colorado switches from pure preference to 50/50 hybrid (half preference queue, half weighted random) starting 2028.
3. **MT combo prices** include deer/elk tag + fishing + upland bird. Prerequisites ($67.50 NR) must be purchased separately.
4. **ID 2026 change**: Nonresident general season deer/elk tags moved from OTC/FCFS to mandatory draw (Dec application).
5. **WY special price** licenses are significantly more expensive but enter a smaller applicant pool (often better odds).
6. **WA** is unique: no NR quota at all; R/NR compete in same pool. Also notable for very high NR application fee ($152.30 vs $9.61 resident).
7. **CA elk** is extremely limited (~362 tags statewide) with only 1 NR elk tag per year.
8. **OR** changed all Eastern Oregon deer to controlled-only starting 2026. OTC deer only available in Western Oregon.
9. **NV** has a 7-year waiting period after drawing a bull elk tag before you can reapply.
10. **UT** has a 5-year waiting period after drawing limited-entry deer or elk.

## Draw Type Legend

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Lottery** | Pure random draw, every applicant has equal odds regardless of history |
| **Preference** | Ordered queue: highest point holders draw first, zero randomness until tie-breaking |
| **Bonus** | Weighted random: more points = more entries, but never guaranteed |
| **Hybrid** | Some combination: often X% preference queue + Y% weighted/pure random |
